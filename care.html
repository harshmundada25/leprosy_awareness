Daily Care at Anandwan

Comprehensive support for leprosy patients through rehabilitation and medical care.

1. Holistic Skin Care & Hygiene

Anandwan provides specialized care to prevent wounds and infections in patients with reduced sensation.

  • Community-driven hygiene workshops educate patients on proper skin care.
  • Daily supervised cleansing with medicated soaps to prevent infections.
  • Free access to moisturizers and antiseptics to maintain skin health.

2. Advanced Wound Management

At Anandwan, trained caregivers ensure effective wound care to prevent ulcers.

  • Specialized wound care centers provide daily dressing changes.
  • Availability of antibiotic ointments and sterile supplies.
  • Doctors monitor non-healing ulcers and provide advanced treatments.

3. Preventing Deformities Through Rehabilitation

Anandwan offers physiotherapy and mobility support to prevent deformities.

  • Customized splints and braces are provided to patients.
  • Regular physiotherapy sessions help strengthen muscles.
  • Workshops teach daily exercises for mobility improvement.

4. Eye Protection & Vision Care

Anandwan offers free eye checkups and protective eyewear to leprosy patients.

  • Regular eye screenings to prevent complications.
  • Free sunglasses and lubricating eye drops provided.
  • On-site ophthalmologists available for consultations.

5. Nutritional Support & Diet Planning

A well-balanced diet is essential for healing and immunity.

  • Nutritious community meals provided to all patients.
  • Regular distribution of protein and vitamin supplements.
  • Nutritionists offer personalized diet plans.

6. Mental & Emotional Well-being

Anandwan provides a supportive community to combat social stigma.

  • Dedicated counseling sessions for emotional support.
  • Engagement in skill-building workshops for self-reliance.
  • Access to group therapy and community events.

7. Regular Medical Checkups

Leprosy is treatable, and Anandwan ensures patients receive continuous medical supervision.

  • Free access to multi-drug therapy (MDT) and follow-ups.
  • Weekly doctor consultations for all patients.
  • Emergency care and specialized treatment centers for complications.
